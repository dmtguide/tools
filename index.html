Juice Mixer
× How to Use Juice Mixer

Calculates ingredient amounts for your desired PG/VG blend.

Set total volume, active ingredient ratio, and PG/VG split using the top three sliders. The active ratio can also be typed in manually.

To mix by a specific ingredient weight without knowing the final volume, adjust the bottom slider or type directly into the active ingredient field.

The solubility indicator shows whether your active ingredient will dissolve fully in the available PG — active ingredients are insoluble in VG, so always dissolve in PG first, then add VG.

PG and VG are easier to measure by weight — gram values are shown alongside ml values in all output fields.
